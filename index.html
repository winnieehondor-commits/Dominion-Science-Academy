A Legacy of Excellence in Ondo State

Established in 2004 in Owo, Dominion Science Academy has grown into a reputable institution for both primary and secondary education. We are dedicated to the holistic development of every child, blending rigorous science education with strong moral foundations.

Our Pillars of Success

Academic Excellence

Driven by a curriculum that emphasizes mathematics, science, and rigorous preparation for top university entrance exams like WAEC and JAMB.

Moral & Spiritual Growth

A foundation of Christ-centered teaching ensuring students develop sound character and moral integrity, built upon our motto: "On Christ We Build."

Discipline & Leadership

We instill the discipline recognized by the Ondo State Ministry of Education, shaping tomorrow's responsible, confident, and ethical leaders.
