Discover the story, mission, and dedication behind Dominion Science Academy.

Our Story

Dominion Science Academy was **established in 2004 by Mr. Hassan Shaka** with the vision of creating an institution where academic rigour meets Christ-centered moral training. Starting small, we have steadily grown to include vibrant primary and secondary sections in Owo, Ondo State.

We are fundamentally committed to **holistic development**, ensuring every student is well-equipped spiritually, intellectually, and morally to face future challenges. Our focus remains sharp: producing disciplined, top-tier scholars.

Dominion Science Academy Campus

Our Nurturing Environment

Our state-of-the-art facilities, including a dedicated **boarding house**, are designed to foster learning, creativity, and spiritual growth. We believe in providing a safe, inclusive, and warm environment where every one of our **over 300 students** can thrive.

Our Core Foundation

Motto: "On Christ We Build"

Vision

To be the premier institution in Nigeria, recognized for graduating Christ-centered leaders distinguished by discipline and academic excellence.

Mission

To provide a holistic, science-focused education rooted in strong Christian values and exemplary discipline.

Key Goals:

  • Achieve 100% distinction rate in core science subjects in external exams.
  • Maintain the highest standards of student discipline and conduct.
  • Foster a deep sense of moral and spiritual responsibility.
  • Ensure successful admission of graduates into competitive universities.

Practical Science in Action

Performance Pillars

Academic Excellence

We pride ourselves on our consistent **high performance in WAEC and JAMB**. Our curriculum is intentionally designed to place strong emphasis on **mathematics and core sciences**, preparing our students not just to pass exams, but to excel and succeed in tertiary education and beyond.

Focused Learning

Discipline and Moral Development

Our core value of **Discipline** is why we have been specially recognized by the **Ondo State Ministry of Education**. Coupled with our **Christ-centered education**, we ensure students leave Dominion Science Academy as responsible, ethical, and morally sound individuals.
